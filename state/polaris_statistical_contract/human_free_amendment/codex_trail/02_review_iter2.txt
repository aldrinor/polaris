## §0. What this amendment changes (scope)

v3.3 + the Phase-0a.0 deliverables put **human SMEs** in load-bearing roles: claim construction, blinded "is it fabricated?" labeling, severity adjudication, tiebreak, and Gate E (human inter-rater reliability — the validity gate that makes Gates A/B/D interpretable). The no-human constraint removes all of these. This amendment replaces them with a **mechanical-oracle + deterministic-hazard + cross-family-LLM** model and **re-scopes the safety claim accordingly**.

This is a **contract amendment**, not a deliverable cleanup: it changes the meaning of Gate E and the entire validity story, so it supersedes the affected v3.3 sections and 6 deliverables.

## §1. The terminology fix (Codex P0)

The single most important honesty correction:

- POLARIS **can** make **fabrication status** a true benchmark label (mechanical, source-grounded mutation).
- POLARIS **cannot** make the **severity / risk stratum** (clinical or non-clinical) ground truth without humans. It can make severity a **machine-adjudicated risk stratum** (structured-rule-constrained + cross-family-LLM-adjudicated). "Clinical" is reserved for the clinical domain only; the benchmark spans 6 domains, so the generic term is **severity/risk stratum** (Codex P2 — terminology consistency).

So the design is NOT "Job 1 mechanical, Job 2 LLM." It is:

> **Fabrication status is mechanical. Severity is mostly provenance-constrained and only partly LLM-adjudicated.**

This phrasing minimizes how much of the safety claim rests on LLM judgment, and it is the phrasing the claim license (§6) enforces.

## §2. The three-layer model (replaces the SME construction+adjudication stack)

### Layer 1 — Mechanical fabrication oracle (the truth backbone)
Real source claim → **deterministic mutation** → KNOWN fabricated / non-fabricated label. We know the label because we injected it. The core measurement — *"did the verifier miss a planted fabrication?"* — depends on **no LLM judge**. Anti-circularity is structural: the dependent variable is mechanical.

- Prefer **structured sources** where truth is a field value: clinical-trial registries (NCT / EudraCT), drug labels with structured dose/contraindication/boxed-warning fields, DOI'd papers with extractable numbers.
- Mutation is auditable: original value, mutated value, source field, mutation type, diff, and the regenerated expected label are all logged (`mutation_audit_log`).

### Layer 2 — Deterministic hazard scaffold (rules first, before any LLM)
The **source field + mutation type** assign a presumptive risk band WHERE RULES CAN DECIDE: dose, contraindication, boxed warning, withdrawal status, pediatric population, pregnancy warning, endpoint direction, mortality/safety outcome. **Codex P0 caveat — severity laundering**: a "dose field changed" rule yields **S0-candidate**, NOT auto-S0. The rule proves a safety-relevant field was altered; final S0 still needs the explicit S0 predicate test (0a.-1.B) applied. Layer 2 narrows the plausible band; it does not certify S0.

### Layer 3 — Cross-family LLM severity adjudication (residual judgment only)
For severity that rules cannot fully determine, OR to confirm/challenge the Layer-2 band, a **panel of ≥3 different-family LLMs** adjudicates. **Used only for severity, never for fabrication status.**

### §3.1 Confirmatory vs exploratory separation (Codex P1 — fabrication status is NEVER LLM-judged)

The core anti-circularity guarantee is that **fabrication STATUS is never LLM-judged**. Therefore:

- **Confirmatory gold set** (feeds Gates A/B/D, the UCB bound, the headline claim): contains **ONLY mechanically-labeled status items** — the 5 strong types + the citation_swap/scope_overreach subtypes whose unsupportedness is created by **controlled source/field mismatch AND deterministically proven** (§5 E1 no-accidental-support). No LLM-status items, ever.
- **Exploratory set** (separately licensed, never feeds Gates A/B/D or the bound): any fab item whose status is NOT mechanically certain. Reported as exploratory observation only, under its own weaker license. An LLM may *flag* such items, but that flag never becomes a confirmatory label.

**Binding rule**: a fab item enters the confirmatory gold set iff its unsupportedness is created by controlled source/field mismatch **and** E1 deterministically proves the cited packet cannot support the mutated proposition. Otherwise it is exploratory-only. There is NO "LLM-panel-flagged status" path into the confirmatory measurement.

## §4. Cross-family panel + anti-circularity (Codex)

- **≥3 families**: one GPT/Codex-family, one Claude-family, one independent third (Qwen or other in the POLARIS stack). 2 is the minimum useful; 3 lets us detect one-family outliers + compute meaningful agreement.
- **Independence controls**: generator-family ≠ verifier-family ≠ judge-family where feasible (extends the contract's existing two-family rule); judges do NOT see verifier outputs/rationales/scores/labels; freeze prompts, rubrics, model versions, decoding settings, source packets before evaluation; store judge first-pass labels before any consensus; include adversarial controls (known status, intentionally-ambiguous severity); **report disagreement, not just consensus**.
- **The structural protection**: fabrication status is not LLM-judged at all — the LLM panel only affects severity stratification.

## §5. Gate E → E1 + E2 (the validity story, reframed)

Human Gate E ("are trained humans applying the rubric consistently enough that Gates A/B/D are interpretable?") no longer exists. Replace with TWO gates:

### Gate E1 — Mechanical Oracle Integrity (the new validity backbone)
Checks that the benchmark labels are actually KNOWN:
- source-fetch reproducibility, structured-field extraction correctness, mutation audit logs, original-vs-mutated diff validity, **no accidental unsupported ORIGINAL claims**, deterministic expected-label regeneration, fixture hash stability.
- **Packet-level "no-accidental-support" check (Codex P1)**: for every confirmatory item, deterministically verify that NO source in the cited packet supports the mutated proposition (not just that the swapped/narrowed source is disjoint). For citation_swap/scope_overreach this is the gate that promotes an item from exploratory to confirmatory. A packet that could accidentally support the "fabrication" invalidates the item.
- **LLM-extracted facts are NOT mechanical (Codex P2)**: a source fact counts as mechanical-truth ONLY if it is backed by a deterministic structured field OR exact-span reproducibility. An LLM-paraphrased/LLM-extracted "original value" is NOT a mechanical anchor and cannot ground a confirmatory label.
- This is what makes Gates A/B/D interpretable for fabrication miss-rate. **If E1 fails, the whole benchmark is invalid** (we don't actually know the labels).

### Gate E2 — Machine Severity Stability (the reframed agreement gate)
- AC1 / Krippendorff across the LLM judges, per-stratum disagreement, family-specific bias checks, deterministic-guardrail override rates, consensus-vs-rule conflict rates.
- **Gate A/B/D survival under E2 failure (Codex P1 — precise)**: Gate A/B/D severity stratification now uses **machine-adjudicated severity** (Layer-2 scaffold + Layer-3 panel), NOT SME-adjudicated severity (the v3.3 SME reference is amended out). On E2 PASS: the full severity-stratified Gate A/B/D results + their UCB bounds are confirmatory. On E2 FAIL: only the **severity-agnostic overall fabrication miss-rate** (E1-backed, mechanical labels) remains confirmatory; all **per-severity-stratum** claims (S0/S1/S2-specific bounds) drop to exploratory and the claim license forbids any severity-stratified assertion. The reporting template emits the E2 verdict alongside every stratified number.
- **Agreement-worship guard (Codex)**: high AC1 among LLM judges can mean consistent shared bias. E2 is a STABILITY signal, not a TRUTH signal — labeled as such.

## §6. Claim license (binding — forbids overclaim)

**CAN claim**:
> "POLARIS achieved a verifier miss-rate bounded at X% on a reproducible benchmark of deterministically injected fabrications against fetched cited sources; fabrication-status labels are mechanical, while severity strata are provenance-constrained and cross-family-LLM-adjudicated, not expert-validated."

**Strongest split form**:
> "The fabrication-detection result is mechanically labeled. The severity-stratified result is machine-adjudicated and should be interpreted as a bench safety stress test, not clinical validation."

**CANNOT claim**: any AFFIRMATIVE assertion that the benchmark was "expert-validated", "clinically validated", "physician-reviewed", "expert-grade", or any phrasing implying a human clinician adjudicated.

**Claim-license linter — negation-aware (Codex P2)**: the linter bans the AFFIRMATIVE assertion, NOT the required negated disclaimer. It must PASS "not expert-validated" / "is NOT clinical expert validation" (those are mandatory disclaimers) and FAIL "expert-validated" / "clinically validated" used as a positive claim. Enforcement = match the forbidden phrase only when NOT immediately preceded by a negator ("not", "no", "isn't", "is not", "never"). A naive substring ban would block the mandatory disclaimer — that is itself a defect.

**The hard line in the contract**:
> Human-free POLARIS validation is a reproducible bench test of verifier behavior under mechanically-known fabrications. It is NOT clinical expert validation. Severity labels are machine-adjudicated risk strata and must be reported as such.

## §7. Self-deception guardrails (Codex — clinical-safety context: a fake-looking validation is worse than an honest bench test)

1. **Benchmark over-templating**: if mutations are too formulaic, the verifier may learn benchmark artifacts, not real fabrication detection. Mitigation: mutation-template diversity quota + held-out template families + report transfer gap.
2. **Source-selection bias**: structured sources are easier to validate than messy clinical prose; performance may not transfer to unstructured papers/guidelines/ambiguous claims. Mitigation: disclose the structured/unstructured split + report per-source-class miss-rate; do NOT generalize a structured-source number to all prose.
3. **Severity laundering** (§2 Layer-2): "dose field changed" → S0-**candidate**, never auto-S0.
4. **Citation-swap illusion** (§3): replacing a citation proves unsupportedness only when the mismatch is mechanically guaranteed (disjoint entity).
5. **Scope-overreach ambiguity** (§3): causal/clinical overreach is judgment, not mechanical — don't launder it as mechanical.
6. **Judge contamination** (§4): shared examples/rationales across generator/verifier/judge prompts → agreement reflects shared conditioning, not independent validation.
7. **Agreement-worship** (§5): high LLM-judge AC1 = stability, not truth.
8. **Clinical claim creep** (§6): the final report must not let "safety/clinical/expert validation" sneak back in via phrasing.
