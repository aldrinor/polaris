## 16-dimension visual audit checklist

For each dimension, return PASS / PARTIAL / FAIL with a one-line
verdict.

1. **Visual hierarchy** — eyebrow → H1 → subtitle → proof-as-CTA → primary CTA.
   Is the eye drawn through that order without backtracking?
2. **Typographic system** — display H1 weight, leading, tracking. Are
   the body/sub/eyebrow tiers genuinely distinct or muddy?
3. **Whitespace rhythm** — vertical spacing between blocks. Is it
   confident or anxious (gaps too small/big)?
4. **Color discipline** — brand-red (#c8102e) appears ONLY at the
   primary CTA + eyebrow + footer link + "See the full proof" arrow.
   Verified-green appears on the matched-stamp + numerics.
   Anything else?
5. **Numeric verification visceral** — are the bolded-green numerics
   inside the claim sentence ACTUALLY proving verification at a
   glance? Or are they reading as decoration?
6. **Proof-as-CTA framing** — is the card legibly the HERO climax (not
   "a card below the fold")? Card elevation (`shadow-card`), rounded
   corners, ring? Does it earn its real estate?
7. **One-CTA discipline** — exactly one primary action. Is "Try a
   verified brief →" sufficiently dominant?
8. **Honest signature pill** — the small "⬡ signed bundle" pill (or
   "⊟ signature attached" / "⊠ not signed" depending on tri-state).
   Reads as a real cryptographic affordance, not a marketing badge?
9. **Source pill** — "T1 source" top-right of the card. Reads as
   evidence tier, not visual noise?
10. **Honest sovereignty wording** — subtitle says "Canadian-hosted...
    built toward sovereign Canadian deployment" — present-tense
    "sovereign" overclaim absent? Footer disclosure says "LLM inference
    is currently routed via OpenRouter (US), disclosed at /transparency"
    — honest?
11. **Footer information density** — does the footer feel substantive
    (brand mark + provenance + disclosure + navigation) without
    cluttering?
12. **Mobile fidelity** (390×844) — does the H1 still feel display-
    weight? Does the proof-as-CTA card render the full claim without
    awkward truncation? Is the primary CTA still thumb-target sized?
13. **Tablet fidelity** (768×1024) — intermediate width: are
    proportions confident or stretched/compressed?
14. **Trust signals integrated** — Canadian-hosted mark + signed-bundle
    pill + transparency footer link — do they cohere into a single
    trust story, or read as 3 disconnected stickers?
15. **Frontier comparison** — open Perplexity / ChatGPT DR / Linear in
    your mind. Where does this beat them visually? Where does this
    fall short visually?
16. **Carney first-3-seconds** — if PM Carney opens this with no
    context, does the proof-as-hero actually communicate the
    differentiator (per-sentence provability), or does he need a
    paragraph of explanation?
