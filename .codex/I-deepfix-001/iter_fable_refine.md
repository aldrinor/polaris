## real_architecture

End-to-end: PLAN -> RETRIEVE -> CONSOLIDATE -> SYNTHESIZE (coherent prose FIRST) -> ATTRIBUTE (deterministic binding, writer tokens as hints) -> VERIFY (per-sentence, downstream) -> REPAIR -> RENDER. Global coherence is produced ONCE, at COMPOSE time, over basket groups and the cross-basket section plan; per-sentence verification runs DOWNSTREAM as a filter that drops/hedges/re-attributes/repairs a sentence but never re-fragments prose and is never the compositor. Composition granularity (paragraph/section) is decoupled from certification granularity (sentence) — the WebWeaver/TTD-DR/LongCite convergent split. (External-cite correction: arXiv 2604.01432 is unconfirmed and dropped; the claim rests on the in-repo root analysis and the reviewed briefs.)

1. **PLAN.** `_section_baskets_for_compose(section, credibility_analysis)` (`multi_section_generator.py:4927`) is the outline-node->evidence binding. Upgrade it to carry (facet, basket-ids, pro/con allocation), fed by `expert_facet_planner.py` (counter_evidence lens at `:44`).

4. **SYNTHESIZE — coherence born here, nowhere downstream.** Per section the writer drafts one coherent multi-sentence narrative per multi-source basket GROUP; cross-basket analytical units and the section lead drafted in the same pass, from supplied evidence only.
5. **ATTRIBUTE — deterministic binding is the authority (ADOPTED from Codex).** Writer-emitted `[#ev:id:start-end]` tokens are hints. The binding authority is `verify_sentence_provenance` + the bounded re-anchor (`provenance_generator.py:1297-1352`, no new acceptance path) + the argmax span resolver (`PG_SPAN_RESOLVER`, `:1349-1352`); the compose loop keeps the verifier's re-pointed sentence (`verified_compose.py:1286-1289`).
